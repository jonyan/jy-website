Yahoo Internship

June 2014

Note: The products I worked on have not yet been released. Below is a general description of my tasks and experiences.

I spent my sophomore year summer at Yahoo as a product intern within the Communications team under Sol Lipman. I had the exciting opportunity to work on a brand new Yahoo product and see how a new product gets built from the ground up within a large tech company. I sat in on sprint planning meetings between our senior product manager and senior engineering manager and learned how product road maps are developed and implemented. I conducted market research and sat in on product marketing meetings and multiple user studies and learned how to turn quantitative and qualitative data into actionable product features.

I spent a large majority of my time using wire-framing tools like Sketch and pen and paper, designing and exploring various user experiences for several products, focusing specifically on onboarding flows.

I worked across multiple teams to redesign an existing Yahoo product, focusing on simplifying the number of features as well as the interface and retargeting the product at a different market segment. Further, I helped develop a roadmap for the implementation of this redesign.
