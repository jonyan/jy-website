Redesigning MIG

December 2014
The Project

Redesign The Mobile Innovation Group’s website in addition to designing a new logo.

Background

The Mobile Innovation Group is a multi-institutional group of researchers at Stanford, INSEAD and MIT that studies how mobile platforms enable a new set of entrepreneurs and innovative ideas to emerge.

Client is interested in modernizing and simplifying its current website (below) as well as creating a new logotype.

Tools

Sketch
Photoshop/Illustrator
Pen & Paper

The Redesign

The client emphasized that the majority of people who visit their website are from research conferences. The client wanted the website to be a quick easy way for visitors to understand what MIG was as well as for visitors to sign up for the MIG mailing list.

Logo

I designed a logo aiming to convey a sense of modernism and represent The Mobile Innovation Group’s research into mobile technology. The client wanted it to be simple and one color (following Stanford University’s color palatte).

I presented the client with three variations, of which they selected the first.




MIG is now live at mig.stanford.edu. Note: All design's depicted by myself, development by David Jiang.
